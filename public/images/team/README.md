## Image Requirements:

- **Format**: JPG or WebP
- **Aspect Ratio**: 1:1 (square)
- **Resolution**: 400x400px minimum (800x800px recommended for retina)
- **File Size**: Under 200KB each
- **Background**: Neutral or professional setting
- **Lighting**: Good, even lighting
- **Composition**: Head and shoulders, facing camera
- **Expression**: Professional, approachable

## Tips:

- Use consistent background across all photos
- Similar lighting and color tone
- Professional attire
- Compress images using TinyPNG or Squoosh
- Use WebP format for better performance
